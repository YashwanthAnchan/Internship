COMPANIESE OWNED BY BMW


BMW

The BMW, Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.


ROLLS ROYCE

Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited is a British luxury automobile maker that has operated as a wholly owned subsidiary of BMW AG since 2003 – as the exclusive manufacturer of Rolls-Royce-branded motor cars.


MINI-COOPER

MINI, a British automotive brand, founded in Oxford, has been owned by BMW Group for over 20 years.


BMW MOTORRAD

BMW Motorrad is the motorcycle brand and division of German automotive manufacturer, BMW. It has produced motorcycles since 1923.

 BMW MOTORSPORT DIVISION

BMW M Motorsport (formerly BMW Motorsport) is the division of BMW responsible for motorsport-related activities, including works-run competition programmes in touring car racing, sports car racing, motorcycle racing. The current organisation is a result of a restructure in April 2021,[1] bringing together the BMW M high-performance division with the competitive motorsport division. Key personnel include Franciscus van Meel, CEO of BMW M GmbH, and Andreas Roos, Head of BMW M Motorsport.
